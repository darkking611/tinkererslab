Equipments and Sensors

The Tinkerers lab at IIT Patna has a long range of electronics and mechanical components that can be used at the lab itself or can be issued by the subordinators. The Tinkerers lab provides a space for making projects as well as the components required to build projects. It is a place where students from different years from all the departments work under projects like the Robocon Team, B.Tech. projects, Developer Student Club (DSC), Inter IIT Tech Meet, E-yantra, Sparkonics (Electronics Club of IITP), Anwesha (Techno-Cultural fest of IITP), Celesta(Technical Fest of IITP).
