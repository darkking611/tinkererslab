About Us

There is a huge gap between what we learn in lecture halls and what industries really need. Many engineers lack practical knowledge and experience in industry. With the objective to bridge that gap Tinkerers Lab IIT Patna has been established to provide a perfect environment that allows the upcoming engineers to improve their knowledge and skills. Our motive is to provide practical knowledge and hands-on -expereience. The lab is accessible 24X7 throughout the year. This allows students to work as per their comfort. This platform offers complete freedom for Tinkerers to work with their ideas and to mould and develop it into something innovative and useful.

Equipments and Sensors

The Tinkerers lab at IIT Patna has a long range of electronics and mechanical components that can be used at the lab itself or can be issued by the subordinators. The Tinkerers lab provides a space for making projects as well as the components required to build projects. It is a place where students from different years from all the departments work under projects like the Robocon Team, B.Tech. projects, Developer Student Club (DSC), Inter IIT Tech Meet, E-yantra, Sparkonics (Electronics Club of IITP), Anwesha (Techno-Cultural fest of IITP), Celesta(Technical Fest of IITP).
